About Us

The Association of Manufacturers of Light Industry "Alliance Textile" is one of the largest associations in the field of light industry of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The company was established in 2011, combining 5 textile enterprises. Nowadays, Alliance Textile has 6 member textile factories throughout Uzbekistan and six trading houses in Turkey, Bangladesh, Russia, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan. The association's enterprises produce textile products, such as cotton yarn ranging from Ne 6 to Ne 60 – ring spun yarn and rotor spun yarn, severe gauze, cotton wool, severe calico and other high quality materials that meet all international standards.
